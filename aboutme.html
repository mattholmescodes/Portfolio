Hi, I'm Matt!

I am 29 years old, currently living in Liverpool. I currently work as an IT Technician at BT, however I am looking to get into the world of software development. My journey into software development started with a bootcamp course at Manchester Codes, learning both frontend and backend javascript, followed by Harvard's CS50X course, which focused on C, Python and Javascript. My favourite programming languages are Javascript and Python, with a particular interest in flask and js animations.

My Hobbies:

My main hobbies are video editing and engaging with music, both as a listener and as a musician. I play guitar and drums, the former being self taught and the latter being a grade 8 standard. Outside of music, I also enjoy gaming and esports as a whole, with a particular interest in Call of Duty, Rocket League and EAFC. I also like traditional sports - I am a big football fan, following Sheffield Wednesday for nearly 25 years and also going to watch local Non-League football when I can.
